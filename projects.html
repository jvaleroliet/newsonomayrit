SONO MAYRIT

Sono Mayrit is a records and tapes collector focused on the music of the Western Mediterranean Sea, researching the connections and influences between Andalusian and North African music. Exploring the rich Flamenco musical heritage, shaped by centuries of cultural exchange and resistance, his selections look into the diverse traditions that built this tragic art form.
